Watch Party

Holaaaaa! to all series-anime freaks out there. Are you ready to face our challenges?? Then, this event is especially for you guys!!!!! This is a fun filled event which brings back nostalgic moments of our favourite series which entertained us in our lockdown.
The objective of the event is to walk you back down the memory lane, to revisit favorite characters and the moments which made us smile and cry!

Round 1: POWER IS POWER

  • This round will be conducted on Quizziz platform
  • One player from each team need to participate.
  • Questions will be from both series and anime
  • Winners will be announced and can go to next round

Round 2: THIS IS WAR

  • This round is one v one
  • The selected teams from round 1 will be made to compete against among teams
  • This will be a knock-out round
  • The winners will move to next round

Round 3: DATTEBAYO

  • The winners from round 2 will compete in this final round
  • Further instructions will be notified in telegram group
